Questions & Answers

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO SHIP FROM USA TO TAIWAN?

A. New York (NYC) to Keelung (KEE) & KahShiung (KHH) is around 38 to 45 days.
B. Los Angeles (LA) to Keelung (KEE) & KahShiung (KHH) is around 18 to 23 days.

DO I HAVE SEND MY GOODS TO A DESIGNATED LOCATION OR CAN YOU PICK THEM UP?

We provide both (Door to Door) & (Port to Port) services.
SHiiP can arrange a goods-pick-up service with an extra fee. Or you can send your goods to one of our warehouses in your city.

I ONLY SEE 3 DIMENSIONS FOR THE BOX, BUT WHAT IF I HAVE DIFFERENT DIMENSION THAT IS NOT PROVIDED?

A. We accept any types of dimensions, please feel free to ask us through the website live chat or send us an email with photos of your goods.

B. Suggested companies that provide double wall box. * uline.com * OFFICE DEPOT * UHAUL * Staple * The Home Depot

IS THE PRICE SHOWN ON THE WEBSITE CALCULATOR THE FINAL PRICE?

The price is usually final, but this could be affected depending on case to case. Please, use the live chat or email to get your final confirmation.

WHAT DOCUMENT SHOULD I PREPARE?

Scan of your passport, ID, and packing list.
Note: The packing list will be provided by us

IN ADDITION TO THE COST OF THE WEBSITE QUOTATION, WHAT OTHER COST COULD BE ADDED?

If you choose “Door to Door” service, you also need to pay for the pick-up fee and Taiwan moving fee.
Note: Our social media and website give out discounts all the time, please subscribe to our Email Notification.

I HAVE SOME LARGE LUGGAGES (EX. CAR, BED, OR PIANO), COULD YOUR COMPANY SHIP THEM?

Of course, all you need to provide us is the commodity, dimension, and total weight of the goods.
Please, contact us for more details.

IS THERE A TRACKING STATUS?

Yes, we will provide you updates your goods throughout the delivery. And you may also request for your tracking status through live chat or email.
