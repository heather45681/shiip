HOW IT ALL STARTED

Shiip ForeverBest

SHiiP was founded in 2016 by Terry, David, and Paul who faced the challenges shared by many college graduated students. The closer the graduating date approaches, the more time he found himself spending on packing and shipping rather than spending quality times with friends and on school.

The original idea was simple: Shipping should be as easy as taking a photo. And that’s how it works. Take a photo, send them to us, and we will calculate the shipping rate for you.

The full-service operation - including PICKUP, PACKAGING, and LOW SHIPPING RATES - started in Taipei, Taiwan and now expanded to Los Angeles, Oakland, and New York.

SHiiP is Division of Everbest Logistics Co., Ltd., founded in 1974, located in Zhongshan Dist., Taipei City, have 40 years history.
